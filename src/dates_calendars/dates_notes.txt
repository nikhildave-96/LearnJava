HH / H --- hours in 24 hour time format

hh / h --- hours in 12 hour time format

mm / m --- time in minutes

MM / M --- number of current month

MMM  --- first 3 letters of current month

MMMM  --- full name of current month

dd / d --- day of month

DD / D --- day of year

YYYY = yyyy  --- year of the century 

YY = yy  --- current year

ss / s --- time in seconds

sss  --- time in milliseconds

SSS  --- 

aa / aaa  --- time AM or PM
